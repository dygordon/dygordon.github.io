About Us

Waaga-Ga takes grocery to the next level, combining the low cost and healthfulness of whole foods from the grocery store with the ready-to-eat convenience of food service.

Healthy

Based on superfood building blocks like kale, sweet potato, smoked fish and fresh fruit, with minimal processing and no added chemicals or transfats.

Affordable

By preparing food in quick and tasty ways, we keep costs to the minimum. We also work with the seasons, getting peak flavour and nutrition out of every penny.

Convenient

Imagine if you could buy the same healthy foods you'd prepare for yourself at home, already cooked by trained chefs following the most delicious recipes and ready-to-eat, in our shop, at the park, or off your own table.
